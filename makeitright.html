Motivation

Mumbai is known to be one of the most traffic heavy cities in India. Mumbai’s vehicle density was the highest in the country with 430 cars/km in 2016. The bicycle is a simple solution to some of the world’s most complicated problems. The bicycle is the most efficient form of human transportation. Cycling can combat climate change, ease of urban congestion, and build human fitness. It brings us together, and also at times allows us to escape. It takes us places we would never see any other way!

Goal

Design a service which would encourage people to cycle for non-recreational purposes.
