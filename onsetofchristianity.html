The onset of christianity : Design of a lesson in local history

General learning objective

To make students aware of their religious history so as to be able to identify the causes of influences/change, the extent of influence/change in the society and their results.

Student learning objective

#1 Given the map of the Chota Nagpur plateau region(condition), students(audience) should be able to identify(behavior) the influenced areas and associated events by the christian missionaries(degree).

#2 Given a particular event in history(condition), student(audience) should be able to question(behavior) the occurrence of the event (degree).

#3 Given a change in one aspect of life(condition) , students(audience) should be able to identify(behavior) cultural changes in society(degree).
